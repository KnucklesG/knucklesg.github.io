GLASS LATHE

Building a custom glassblowing lathe from the ground up.
◐ IN PROGRESS
// ABOUT THIS BUILD //

I'm designing and building a custom glassblowing lathe from scratch. Nothing on the market does exactly what I need at a price that makes sense — so I'm making my own.

The goal is a variable-speed, near-silent lathe with a long enough bed for large pieces and a modular torch mount. Every step of the build is being filmed.

This is the biggest fabrication project I've taken on. Follow along on Patreon for the full video series and behind-the-scenes.
