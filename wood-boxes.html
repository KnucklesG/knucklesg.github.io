// ABOUT THIS COLLAB //

My pops is a master craftsman with decades of woodworking experience. Together we developed a process to make stash boxes that fit your glass piece exactly — not approximately, exactly.

We scan the piece, model the interior cavity, and cut it to fit. The result is a box where your piece sits perfectly — protected, displayed, and ready to travel.
