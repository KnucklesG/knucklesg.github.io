Got the headstock fully assembled this week. The spindle runs true with almost no wobble — well within acceptable tolerance for flameworking speeds.

Spent a couple of days dialing in the bearing preload. Too tight and it runs hot, too loose and you get chatter on the glass. Found the sweet spot.

![Headstock assembly](../../images/lathe-headstock.jpg)

Next up is the tailstock and the motor mount bracket. Should have that done within the next two weeks.
